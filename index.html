Services

Haircuts

Whether you’re looking for a quick clean-up or a fresh new look, haircuts at Whitney’s are all about listening to what you want and making it work for your everyday life. You can walk in, relax, and leave feeling confident and taken care of.

Coloring Services

Colorings are personalized and thoughtful, never rushed. From subtle changes to bold transformations, appointments give your stylist the time needed to create a look that fits you and keeps your hair healthy.

Hair Styling

Styling at Whitney’s is perfect for when you want to look polished and put-together, whether it’s for an event or just because. It’s an easy way to treat yourself and walk out feeling ready for whatever’s next..

Our Pricing

At Haircuts at Whitney’s, we aim to keep pricing competitive while still delivering quality work. Haircuts follow a general range, while color and styling prices are set by each independent stylist based on experience, technique, and the level of customization.

Haircuts

Most haircut services typically range from $21–$28.

Color & Styling Services

Color and styling services are priced individually by each stylist. For the most accurate quote, we recommend checking with your stylist before your appointment.

Note: Additional services and their prices may vary by stylist.
